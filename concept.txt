Thing explains that the user will be presented with a sentence with a gap
They will have to type in a word to fill the gap

Each sentence will have its own page
Each word will be saved as an object
Then at the end in a text widget the thing will be put together

First page will have explanation and continue button
Next four pages will have a sentence label, an entry box and a continue button
Each entry will be saved as an object, if they enter nothing, an output will
tell them to enter something and not let them pass
The sixth window will have a text widget showing all they have entered and asking
them if they wish to continue or exit the app.
